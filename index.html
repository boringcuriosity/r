Why?

Removing design discussions and streamlining our build processes allows these projects to be faster and cheaper.

We came up with Bare Necessities as a response to the prevalence of overpriced template based websites. Every website deserves, if not requires, to have its own voice. Current technologies allow for us to design unique websites even on a restricted budget and a tight deadline; all it takes is years of experience.

Aesthetics are subjective and influenced by each person's experiences. We make conscious decisions when designing websites. Changing parts of a design in response to comments might, and often will, break the cohesion of the overall design. The website becomes a washed out mixture of ideas.
